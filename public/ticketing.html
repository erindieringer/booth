What is the name of one of the books on the bookshelf? (choose both or one)

Correct!

From the Old World to the New and Mail Steamer Went Down In The Midatlantic

Titanic passenger William Thomas Stead wrote both of these books decades before he set foot onboard as a passenger. Legend has it these stories were based on a vision he had in the 1880’s and tell stories very similar to what happened to this ship! In one a White Star Line vessel had to come to the rescue of a sister vessel after it struck an iceberg and in the other a mail steamer wrecked and couldn’t save all of the passengers due to a lack of lifeboats.
