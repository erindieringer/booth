Ballroom

People bustle around the ballroom, dancing to and enjoying the sound of the band. What song are they playing?

Correct!

Songe d’Automne

While it isn’t known for certain, this song is likely one of the ones played by the ship’s 8-person band as the vessel was sinking, in an effort to calm the passengers. The band played all night, even after it was clear the ship was going to sink, and went down with the ship.
